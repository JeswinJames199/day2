Trivandrum

Trivandrum, also known as Thiruvananthapuram, is the capital city of Kerala, India. It is known for its beautiful landscapes, historic landmarks, and rich cultural heritage.

Explore the magnificent attractions and experience the vibrant culture of this city.

A gracious and evergreen city spread over seven hills, Thiruvananthapuram, capital of Kerala, is steeped in history and culture. Located in the extreme south of the state, its shores are washed by the warm waters of the Arabian Sea.

This is a city where time moves gracefully with the day unfolding according to time honoured rhythms, qualities which make it one of the best cities in the country.
